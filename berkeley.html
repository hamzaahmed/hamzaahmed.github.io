UC Berkeley
My journey as a student at the best public university in the world
Coming from Saudi Arabia, Berkeley was definitely not what I was used to. There was a lot I had to get used to before I felt comfortable at Berkeley. Fast forward two years, Berkeley feels like a second home.
When I first got here, I had very little programming experience. In high school, I did a little bit of coding in Visual Basic; I developed a replica of the game Deal or No Deal, which was actually was convinced me to pursue a career in Computer Science. In my first two years at Berkeley, I learned several programming languages including Python, Java, C, Scheme, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc. If I had to choose a favorite, it'd definitely be Python, just because its so clean and elegant - I really enjoy coding in it.
I will continue to update this as a I learn more and more at this wonderful place.
